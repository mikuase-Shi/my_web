About Me

Hello! I am Shengshi (Mikuase), an undergraduate student at Peking University (Class of 2024), majoring in Intelligent Science and Technology. I am passionate about Robotics, Embodied AI, Computer Vision, and Machine Learning.

My current research focuses on visual-language-action (VLA) models and reinforcement learning (RL) in robotic manipulation. I am also an active participant in competitive programming (C++/Python) and enjoy building personal knowledge management systems.

My goal is to pursue a Ph.D. in robotics and AI in the United States starting in 2029.
